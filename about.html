Coffee Trivia

When did coffee first apeared in Rwanda?

Coffee first appeared in Rwanda in 1904, introduced by German missionaries. While the missionaries introduced the crop theoretically in 1893, the first coffee tree was planted in 1904 in Gashonga sector, Rusizi District. Commercial production, however, began later, around the 1930s, under Belgian colonial rule. (source: Sucafina blog )
